\paragraph{Abstract}

The aim of this paper is to provide the rationale driving the
design of the static reflection facility proposed in P0194~\cite{Chochlik-P0194R0}
(and its predecessors N3996~\cite{Chochlik-N3996}, N4111~\cite{Chochlik-N4111},
N4451~\cite{Chochlik-N4451} and N4452~\cite{Chochlik-N4452}),
to enumerate and describe its potential use-cases
and to keep a written record of its evolution. It also answers questions
frequently asked in regard to the proposal.
